SVA req/ack assertions

February 11, 2023 One-minute read
UVM

After req edge, req should be stable and ack is low until ack goes high between 0:11

$rose(req) |-> (req && !ack)[*0:11] ##1 (req && ack)

When ack does high, req should be high as well

!req |-> !ack

req goes low after ack goes low

$fell(req) |-> !ack
