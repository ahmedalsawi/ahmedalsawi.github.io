Stock Market Definitions

December 11, 2020 One-minute read
finance

52 week Range Link to heading

invest Define it as

The 52-week range is a data point traditionally reported by printed financial news media, but more modernly included in data feeds from financial information sources online. The data point includes the lowest and highest price at which a stock has traded during the previous 52 weeks.

Volume Link to heading

Volume is the amount of an asset or security that changes hands over some period of time, often over the course of a day.

Average Volume Link to heading

The Average Volume is the total volume for a specified period divided by the number of bars in that same period.

Market Cap Link to heading

Market capitalization refers to the total dollar market value of a company’s outstanding shares of stock. Commonly referred to as “market cap,” it is calculated by multiplying the total number of a company’s outstanding shares by the current market price of one share.
